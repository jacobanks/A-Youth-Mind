Our purposeOur purpose is to empower the youth of the world produce and share their own stories. We want to cast an empowering light on them by representing their genuine lives with raw photos. By doing so, we can change the negative depictions and polarizing ideaologies that tend to pull apart our world. These youth's representation will no longer be limited to the faces of the outside world.
